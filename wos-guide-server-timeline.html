Server Timeline —
What Unlocks and When

Whiteout Survival operates on a server timeline that progresses through generations and unlocks new content at specific intervals. Understanding where your server is in its timeline lets you plan your hero investments, anticipate upcoming events, and avoid wasting resources on content that is not yet available to you.

Generations

Hero Generation Schedule

New hero generations unlock approximately every 40–80 days depending on your server's age and progression pace. Each generation introduces stronger heroes and new events. Your SvS matchmaking is also influenced by your server's generation — you are generally matched with servers of similar age.

Generation 1

Available from server launch. Heroes: Jeronimo (P2W), Molly, Natalia (P2W), Sergey, Bahiti, Zinman. Hall of Chiefs runs 3 times in Gen 1 featuring Jeronimo. Focus: build Molly and Sergey, save Gold Shards for Gen 2.

Generation 2 (approx Day 40–80)

Heroes: Flint (wheel), Philly (HoC), Alonso. Gold Shards belong on Alonso — aim for 4 stars. Max Flint via Lucky Wheel during Hall of Chiefs only. HoC now features Philly.

Generation 3 (approx Day 120–160)

Heroes: Mia, Logan, and others. Mia is a long-term Lancer investment — her kit stays relevant into Gen 6+. Logan is a strong defensive Infantry option.

Generation 4–6

Reina, Lynn, Gwen, Wayne, Hector introduced across these generations. The meta shifts toward newer heroes — prior investments in Alonso and Mia remain competitive. Continue Lucky Wheel strategy for new wheel heroes.

The two-hero rule: Aim to acquire two heroes per generation through Lucky Wheel spins and saved Legendary Shards. This pace keeps your roster competitive without requiring spending.

Furnace Milestones

Key Furnace Levels and What They Unlock

Furnace 7 — Alliance tab unlocks. Join an alliance immediately.

Furnace 9 — Required for some gift codes. Many resource events become accessible.

Furnace 16 — Foundry Battle unlocks. This is a major milestone — Foundry Battle is one of the highest-value alliance events in the game.

Furnace 19 — The Labyrinth unlocks. Weekly reset event with excellent token rewards including Mithril and Fire Crystal Shards.

Furnace 26 — Advanced combat content becomes available. SvS competition becomes significantly more serious at this level.

Furnace 30 — Target milestone for Fire Crystal content. Fire Crystal upgrades are a major late-game progression system.

Furnace first, always. Every other building and system in the game is gated behind your Furnace level. Never let anything else take priority over your next Furnace upgrade.

Planning Ahead

How to Use the Timeline as F2P

The most common planning mistake is over-investing in a current generation hero when the next generation is close. If Gen 2 is 2–3 weeks away, save your Gold Shards rather than spending them on Gen 1 heroes that will soon be superseded.

The same logic applies to Exclusive Gear — only invest in heroes you are confident will remain relevant for at least 2–3 generations. Alonso, Mia, and Logan are the safest long-term investments in the early-to-mid game.

Watch your alliance's server announcements and the in-game news feed for generation unlock notices. Planning your Hall of Chiefs gem saving around upcoming hero generations is one of the highest-leverage F2P decisions you can make.

Community note: Generation unlock timings vary between servers and change with game updates. The timings in this guide are approximate. Ask senior players in your alliance for your specific server's current generation pace.
